Legal Services Agreement

This Legal Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2024, between:

Client: Acme Corporation
Address: 123 Business Street, New York, NY 10001

And

Attorney: Smith & Associates Law Firm
Address: 456 Legal Avenue, New York, NY 10002

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Attorney agrees to provide legal consultation services related to corporate governance, contract review, and compliance matters.

2. FEES AND PAYMENT
The Client agrees to pay the Attorney a retainer fee of $10,000 upon execution of this Agreement.
Hourly rate for additional services: $350 per hour
Invoices will be submitted monthly and are due within 30 days of receipt.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall commence on January 15, 2024, and continue for a period of six (6) months.
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all information exchanged during the term of this Agreement.

5. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in New York County.
